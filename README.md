# RAJANO
It is a not for profit association focused on Education access, environmental literacy, gender based violence, community climate change resilience, professional and economic insertion for vulnerable youth and women, youth and adult literacy, good governance and civic education. 
Promoting the right to access education as a way to combat the root causes of poverty in rural and vulnerable communities, especially for vulnerable youth, children, girls, and women.
We use education as a central tool to achieve inclusive and sustainable community development, participation, peace, and social cohesion.
